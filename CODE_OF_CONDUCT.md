# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone who wishes to contribute to this shortlink service.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior ‚úÖ

- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

### Unacceptable Behavior ‚ùå

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Sharing others' private information
- Submitting malicious or inappropriate links
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- Pull requests and issues
- Comments and discussions
- All interactions within this repository
- Representing the project in public spaces

## Shortlink-Specific Guidelines

When submitting shortlinks:

1. **Be Honest** - Only submit links you own or have permission to share
2. **Be Appropriate** - No NSFW, malicious, or illegal content
3. **Be Patient** - Review takes time; respect maintainers' schedules
4. **Be Gracious** - Accept feedback and rejection professionally

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
1. Open a private issue or contact maintainers
2. Provide details about the incident
3. Include relevant links/screenshots if possible

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. **Warning** - First offense, minor issues
2. **Temporary ban** - Repeated minor issues or serious first offense
3. **Permanent ban** - Severe violations or repeated serious issues

### Appeals

You may appeal enforcement decisions by:
- Contacting maintainers privately
- Explaining your perspective
- Demonstrating understanding of the issue
